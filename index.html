Our Story

23 YEARS.
ONE STANDARD.

Flexo Carpenter Inc was founded in 2001 by Felix Okafor — a fourth-generation carpenter who trained in his grandfather's workshop before moving to the United States with tools, knowledge, and an absolute refusal to compromise on quality.


Starting from a rented garage in Philadelphia with one workbench and a set of hand tools, Felix built the business piece by piece — exactly as he builds furniture. Today, Flexo operates from a purpose-built 7,200 sq ft workshop with 16 specialist carpenters, joiners and finishers.


Over 1,400 projects later, our standard hasn't changed. Every piece is planned precisely, cut cleanly, joined properly and finished with pride. The work speaks for itself — and it always will.


Work With Us
ZERO COMPROMISE

Every joint, every surface, every finish meets the same exacting standard. We don't cut corners — ever. Not even on the pieces no one sees.

ON-TIME ALWAYS

We quote realistic timelines and keep them. In 23 years we have completed every project on or before the agreed delivery date.

10-YEAR GUARANTEE

All structural work is backed by a full 10-year guarantee. If anything fails in structure or finish, we fix it — no charge, no questions asked.

SUSTAINABLY SOURCED

All timber is sourced from certified sustainable forests. Good craft and good stewardship of the planet are inseparable to us.
